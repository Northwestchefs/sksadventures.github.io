Community Guidelines

These guidelines outline expectations for participation in SKS Adventures community spaces, including Discord channels and tabletop game sessions.

Respectful Play

Treat all players, moderators, and game masters with courtesy. Communicate clearly, listen actively, and avoid behavior that disrupts the table or community environment.

Inclusive Community

SKS Adventures is committed to an inclusive and welcoming environment. Discrimination, hateful language, or exclusionary behavior is not acceptable.

Collaborative Storytelling

Campaigns and one-shots are shared storytelling experiences. Respect spotlight balance, support other players' character moments, and work toward party cooperation.

Harassment Policy

Harassment, intimidation, threats, or repeated unwanted contact in Discord, direct messages, voice chat, or game sessions is prohibited. Violations may result in warnings, removal from sessions, or permanent bans from community spaces.

Fair Play Expectations

Use honest rolls and transparent communication during sessions. Do not exploit tools, falsify character information, or intentionally undermine table agreements and safety boundaries.
