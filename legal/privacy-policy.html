Privacy Policy

SKS Adventures respects your privacy and is committed to protecting personal information shared by visitors. This Privacy Policy explains what information may be collected when you use this website and how that information may be used.

Information We Collect

This website may collect limited information, including:

  • Basic technical information such as IP address, browser type, and device information.
  • Analytics data used to understand how visitors use the site.
  • Voluntary information submitted through contact forms or email.

Personal information is only collected when you choose to provide it voluntarily.

How We Use Information

Collected information may be used to:

  • Respond to questions or inquiries.
  • Improve website content and performance.
  • Organize community activities such as Dungeons & Dragons games.
  • Understand how visitors interact with the site.

Cookies and Analytics

SKS Adventures may use cookies or analytics tools provided by Google to better understand website traffic and improve user experience. Cookies may store small pieces of information on your device.

Third-Party Services

This website may link to or use third-party services, including:

  • Google services used for analytics or webmaster tools.
  • GitHub Pages used for website hosting.
  • Discord links used for community communication.

These services operate under their own privacy policies.

Data Sharing

SKS Adventures does not sell personal data to third parties.

Data Retention

Personal information is retained only as long as reasonably necessary to respond to inquiries or support community activities.

User Rights

Visitors may request removal of personal information they have submitted.

Contact Information

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or would like to request removal of personal information, please contact us at contact.sksadventures@gmail.com.

Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes to the website. Updates will be posted on this page.
