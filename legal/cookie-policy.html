Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy describes how SKS Adventures may use cookies and similar technologies on this website.

What Cookies Are

Cookies are small text files saved on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember settings, understand usage patterns, and improve the browsing experience.

How Cookies Are Used

SKS Adventures may use cookies to support site functionality, improve performance, and better understand how visitors interact with pages and resources.

Analytics Cookies

This website may use analytics services provided by Google. These services may set cookies that collect non-identifying usage information, such as pages visited, session duration, and referral sources, to help improve site content and usability.

Managing Cookies

Most web browsers allow you to control or disable cookies through settings. Disabling cookies may affect how some pages or features function. For details, review your browser's help documentation.
