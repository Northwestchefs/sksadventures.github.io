Safety Considerations

This page outlines how SKS Adventures builds a respectful, inclusive, and emotionally safe tabletop environment.

Core Standards

  • Respect for each player is mandatory; harassment and discrimination are never tolerated.
  • Consent-first storytelling means no one is pressured into uncomfortable themes.
  • Boundaries are treated as table rules, not suggestions.

Session Zero Agreements

  • We establish lines (hard limits) and veils (fade-to-black topics) before campaign play.
  • Expectations for tone, conflict style, and party cooperation are discussed openly.
  • Players confirm communication preferences, accessibility needs, and pronouns.

In-Session Safety Tools

  • Any player may pause the game at any time to clarify or redirect a scene.
  • Active check-ins are used after intense scenes and at session close.
  • Private messaging is always available if a concern is easier to share one-on-one.

Conflict Resolution & Follow-Up

  • Concerns are handled promptly and discreetly by the DM.
  • Behavior that repeatedly breaks table boundaries may lead to removal from the game.
  • Aftercare and debrief options are offered when emotionally heavy content appears.

If you have specific content concerns, share them in your application or by direct message before Session Zero.
