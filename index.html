What to Expect

  • RP/Combat balance: narrative-forward play with meaningful tactical encounters.
  • Pacing: consistent momentum with clear goals each session.
  • Collaboration: shared spotlight, party teamwork, and player agency.
  • Attendance: reliable schedule and early communication around absences.
  • Respectful behavior: inclusive table norms and constructive communication.

Good fit

  • You enjoy roleplay and group storytelling.
  • You communicate respectfully and consistently.
  • You can commit to recurring sessions.

Not a fit

  • You frequently no-show without notice.
  • You prefer spotlight hogging over team play.
  • You disregard table boundaries or consent.
