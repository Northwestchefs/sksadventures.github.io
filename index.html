What to Expect

  • RP/Combat balance: narrative-forward play with meaningful tactical encounters.
  • Pacing: consistent momentum with clear goals each session.
  • Collaboration: shared spotlight, party teamwork, and player agency.
  • Attendance: reliable schedule and early communication around absences.
  • Respectful behavior: inclusive table norms and constructive communication.

Good fit

  • You enjoy roleplay and group storytelling.
  • You communicate respectfully and consistently.
  • You can commit to recurring sessions.

Not a fit

  • You frequently no-show without notice.
  • You prefer spotlight hogging over team play.
  • You disregard table boundaries or consent.

About the DM

Your DM has been running tabletop adventures since the age of 16 and still loves every minute of it. With decades of experience guiding players through epic campaigns, the focus is always on creating sessions that are immersive, organized, and fun for everyone at the table.

Expect thoughtful preparation, engaging worldbuilding, and a style that balances story, roleplay, and exciting encounters. Player choices matter, characters get their moment to shine, and the goal is always the same: build memorable stories together.

Whether you’re brand new to D&D or a longtime player, you’ll find a welcoming table where creativity, teamwork, and good humor are just as important as the roll of the dice.
