Campaigns

Oathbound monster-hunters are vanishing in snow-buried keeps. Uncover a curse tied to moonlit bloodlines and decide who deserves salvation.

Strange lights pulse under frozen seas, calling ships to ruin. Track the signal, bargain with forgotten powers, and keep your crew from turning on each other.

What to Expect

  • Collaborative storytelling with space for every character arc.
  • Roughly 60/40 roleplay-to-combat balance, tuned to player preference.
  • Consistent attendance is important; please communicate schedule conflicts early.
  • Respectful, inclusive table culture—no harassment, hate speech, or bullying.
